# 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Finara — User Personas

> 5 fictional user personas representing diverse financial profiles of Finara's target audience: **Indonesian students and young professionals** navigating debt, budgeting, and cashflow management.

These personas will eventually be seeded into Supabase as test users (with transactions, debts, budgets, etc.) to demonstrate all of Finara's features.

| **Savings** | Rp 200.000 |
| **Currency** | IDR |
| **Risk Tolerance** | High (unaware of risks) |

### 💳 Debts

| Debt | Type | Principal | Balance | Interest Rate | Min. Payment | Due Date | Penalty Rate | Stress Level |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| SPayLater (Shopee) | BNPL | Rp 800.000 | Rp 600.000 | 2.95% /mo | Rp 150.000 | 20th | 5% | 9 |
| GoPay PayLater | BNPL | Rp 500.000 | Rp 450.000 | 2.5% /mo | Rp 100.000 | 10th | 4% | 8 |
| Pinjol (Online Lending App) | Personal Loan | Rp 1.000.000 | Rp 1.200.000 ⚠️ | 10% /mo | Rp 300.000 | 3rd | 15% | 10 |

### 💸 Spending Habits
- **Top categories**: Food & Dining (35%), Shopping (30%), Entertainment (20%), Transport (10%)
- **Pattern**: Heavy impulsive spender on e-commerce and food delivery; borrows from one platform to pay another (debt cycling)

- **Cashflow volatility**: Extreme — depends entirely on when parents send money

### 🧠 Financial Profile Summary
Dewi represents the **highest-risk user** in Finara — a young student trapped in predatory online lending with no income and spiraling debt (note: pinjol balance **exceeds** the principal due to compounding interest). Her stress level is maxed out. Finara's **default probability alerts**, **penalty escalation warnings**, **emergency buffer indicator** ("Unsafe" level), and **K-Means behavioral profiling** (classifying her as a high-risk/impulsive spender) are designed exactly for users like her.

## 🎯 Why These Personas?

Each persona is crafted to exercise different parts of Finara's engine:

| Persona | Key Finara Features Tested |
|---|---|
| **Rina** | BNPL tracking, budget alerts, default probability |
| **Budi** | Multi-debt optimization, strategy comparison (snowball/avalanche/finara) |
| **Siti** | Variable income handling, cashflow constraints, Monte Carlo simulations |
| **Arief** | Mortgage optimization, interest savings projection, disciplined user UX |
| **Dewi** | High-risk detection, pinjol alerts, K-Means profiling, PPO emergency strategy |
